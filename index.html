The Widaad Project


About Us

We are made up of a group of university students and graduates who have come together to organize community-centred events in a bid to do our part in the society. We seek opportunities to make a difference, and at the same time gain new skills through experience.

Our Vision

  • To inspire postive change in youths locally
  • To lend a hand in upgrading communities overseas

Our Mission

  • To be on the ground making changes
  • To fulfill the needs of chosen communities
  • To empower through knowledge
